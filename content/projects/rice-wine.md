# ğŸ”¬ Fermentation Process

**Step 1: Prepare the Rice**
1. Wash glutinous rice thoroughly
2. Soak overnight in cold water
3. Steam until fully cooked but not mushy
4. Cool to lukewarm (30-35Â°C)

**Step 2: Add Starter Culture**
1. Crush rice wine starter ball into powder
2. Mix thoroughly with cooled rice
3. Transfer to sterilized jar
4. Make a small well in center for liquid to collect

**Step 3: Fermentation**
1. Cover jar with clean cloth
2. Keep at warm temperature (25-30Â°C)
3. Ferment for 2-3 days
4. Liquid (rice wine) will collect in well

**Step 4: Secondary Fermentation (Optional)**
1. Add filtered water to jar
2. Seal and ferment additional 1-2 weeks
3. Strain and bottle
