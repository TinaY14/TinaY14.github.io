About Egg Waffles

Tips: Egg waffles is one of the popular foods in night stalls. Egg waffles is from Hong Kong, and there are two kinds of egg waffles, either Smiley egg waffles with different fillings or Original flavor egg waffles with ice creams with many toppings for you to choose, such as Taro egg waffles, Western-style egg waffles, chocolate egg waffles, matcha egg waffles, etc.

The ingredients are simple and easy to make. Though egg waffles are yummy, the calories for egg waffles is really high. You shouldn't eat too much, one or two is enough!
