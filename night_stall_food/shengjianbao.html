ℹ️ About Shengjianbao

Shengjianbao are juicy pork buns with a crispy bottom, pan-fried to perfection and sprinkled with sesame and scallions. The filling is savory and the dough is soft yet crunchy on the base.

Tips: Shengjianbao originated in Shanghai and are a beloved breakfast and snack food. The secret to their juiciness is the pork gelatin mixed into the filling, which melts during cooking to create hot, flavorful broth inside. The contrast between the soft, fluffy top and crispy, golden bottom is what makes them special. Be careful when biting - the soup inside is very hot! Best enjoyed fresh from the pan with a touch of vinegar.
