Step 1: Bake the Cake

  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl

  4. Stir in hot water (batter will be thin)
  5. Bake in two 9-inch pans for 30-35 minutes
  6. Cool completely

Step 2: Assemble the Layers

  1. Place one cake layer in a springform pan
  2. Spread softened ice cream evenly on top
  3. Fold in chocolate chips if using
  4. Top with second cake layer
  5. Freeze for 4 hours minimum

Step 3: Make the Ganache

  1. Heat cream until just boiling
  2. Pour over chocolate chips
  3. Let sit 2 minutes, then stir until smooth
  4. Add butter and stir until glossy
  5. Let cool slightly

Step 4: Final Assembly

  1. Remove cake from springform pan
  2. Pour ganache over top
  3. Let it drip down the sides
  4. Freeze for 1 more hour
  5. Let sit at room temperature 10 minutes before slicing
