Douhua is a soft tofu dessert served with sweet syrup or savory toppings. It is silky, smooth, and refreshing, making it a popular treat at night markets.

Tips: Douhua is essentially very soft, fresh tofu with a pudding-like texture. The sweet version is popular as a dessert, while the savory version can be enjoyed as a light meal. It's made from fresh soy milk that's just barely coagulated, creating its signature silky texture. Best enjoyed fresh and warm. It's a healthy, protein-rich option that's easy to digest and perfect for all ages!
