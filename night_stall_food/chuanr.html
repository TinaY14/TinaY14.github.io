Chuanr are grilled meat skewers, often lamb or chicken, seasoned with cumin, chili, and other spices. They are a staple of night markets across China and are enjoyed for their smoky, spicy flavor.

Tips: Chuanr originated from Xinjiang province but became popular throughout China. The key to authentic chuanr is the generous use of cumin and chili powder, which gives it its distinctive flavor. Best enjoyed hot off the grill with friends at night markets. The meat is typically marinated beforehand and grilled over charcoal for that perfect smoky taste.
