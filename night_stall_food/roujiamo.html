Roujiamo is a sandwich of slow-cooked, spiced pork stuffed in a crispy flatbread. It is often called the Chinese hamburger and is a filling, flavorful street food option.

Tips: Roujiamo originates from Shaanxi province and is considered one of the world's oldest sandwiches! The key is the contrast between the crispy, flaky flatbread (baijimo) and the tender, flavorful meat. The pork is slow-braised for hours in aromatic spices until it falls apart. The bread is baked in a traditional clay oven for the perfect texture. Best enjoyed hot and fresh - it's a complete meal in your hands!
