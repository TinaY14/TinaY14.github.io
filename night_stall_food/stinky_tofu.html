Stinky tofu is a fermented tofu snack with a strong aroma and a crispy exterior. It is usually deep-fried and served with chili sauce and pickled vegetables, loved by adventurous eaters.

Tips: Stinky tofu is definitely an acquired taste! The fermentation process gives it its distinctive smell, but the taste is much milder than the aroma suggests. It's crispy on the outside, soft and creamy inside. Each region has its own variation - some steam it, others deep fry it. The key is to eat it hot and fresh with plenty of sauce. It's actually quite healthy due to the fermentation process, which creates beneficial bacteria. Don't judge it by its smell - give it a try with an open mind!
