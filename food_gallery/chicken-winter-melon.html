üç≤ About This Soup

Chicken Winter Melon Soup is a light, nourishing Chinese soup known for its subtle flavors and health benefits. Winter melon absorbs the rich chicken broth beautifully, creating a comforting and hydrating dish perfect for any season.

ü•ò Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs or drumsticks - 500g
  • Winter melon - 500g, peeled and cubed
  • Ginger - 3-4 slices
  • Goji berries - 2 tablespoons (optional)
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms - 4-5, rehydrated
  • Water - 8 cups

Seasonings:

  • Salt - to taste
  • White pepper - ¬º teaspoon
  • Shaoxing wine - 2 tablespoons
  • Green onions - for garnish

üë©‚Äçüç≥ Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces
  2. Peel winter melon and cut into 2-inch cubes
  3. Soak dried mushrooms in warm water for 20 minutes
  4. Slice mushrooms after soaking

Step 2: Blanch the Chicken

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil
  2. Add chicken pieces and blanch for 2 minutes
  3. Drain and rinse under cold water
  4. This removes impurities for a clearer broth

Step 3: Simmer the Soup

  1. Add 8 cups fresh water to a large pot
  2. Add blanched chicken, ginger, and Shaoxing wine
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes

Step 4: Add Winter Melon

  1. Add winter melon cubes to the pot
  2. Add mushrooms and goji berries
  3. Continue simmering for 20-25 minutes
  4. Winter melon should be translucent and tender

Step 5: Season and Serve

  1. Season with salt and white pepper to taste
  2. Garnish with chopped green onions
  3. Serve hot

üí° Cooking Tips

  • Winter Melon: Choose firm melon with white flesh
  • Clear Broth: Blanching chicken is key to clarity
  • Timing: Don't overcook winter melon or it becomes mushy
  • Health Benefits: Great for hydration and cooling in summer
  • Variations: Add corn or carrots for extra sweetness
  • Storage: Keeps well in fridge for 3 days
