ü•ò Ingredients

  • Winter melon - 400g, cut into cubes
  • Ground chicken - 200g
  • Red bell pepper - 1, diced
  • Orange/Yellow bell pepper - 1, diced
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 inch piece, minced
  • Soy sauce - 2 tablespoons
  • Oyster sauce - 1 tablespoon
  • Cooking oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Green onions - for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

üë©‚Äçüç≥ Cooking Instructions

  1. Cut winter melon into bite-sized cubes and steam for 10 minutes until tender
  2. Heat oil in wok, add minced garlic and ginger, stir-fry until fragrant
  3. Add ground chicken, cook until browned and cooked through
  4. Add diced bell peppers, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes
  5. Add steamed winter melon, soy sauce, and oyster sauce
  6. Stir gently to combine, season with salt and pepper
  7. Garnish with green onions and serve hot with rice

üí° Cooking Tips

  • Winter Melon: Pre-steam the winter melon to ensure it's tender and absorbs flavors better
  • Colorful Presentation: Use different colored peppers for visual appeal
  • Sauce Balance: Adjust soy sauce and oyster sauce to your taste preference
  • Texture: Don't overcook the peppers - they should stay slightly crisp
