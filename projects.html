Kolmogorov-Arnold Network (KAN) Research

Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory

Served as a student volunteer researcher investigating whether the Kolmogorov-Arnold Network (KAN) model, with its unique approach of using learnable edge functions, can provide better interpretability compared to the current SQUID framework for analyzing deep learning outputs in genomic sequences, specifically in understanding cis-regulatory elements and their complex interactions.

Smart Service Gas Pump Design

Toshiba Research Competition

Collaborated with group members to conduct research proposing a Smart Service on gas pumps. Developed a concept for a new design with disposable recyclable plastic materials on the pump handle and integrated an AI service system to assist customers in avoiding direct contact with the gas pump.

Energy Drinks Luminescence Investigation

Final Paper for Research 2

Collaborated with partners to investigate what causes energy drinks to emit luminescence by placing cuvettes containing different energy drink samples into a spectrophotometer to record maximum absorbance and corresponding wavelength. Created graphs showing maximum fluorescence for samples and provided explanations about the luminescence wavelength collected.
