Foodmon is a gamified food collection web application where users can browse new food cards, mark foods as eaten, save favorites, and track their food adventure progress - it's just like collecting Pokemon!

Status: Currently in progress - actively building the app.

‚ú® Features

  • Browse Foods: Explore a diverse collection of foods from various cuisines worldwide
  • Food Cards: Beautiful, cute cards that showcase food photos, names, and rarity levels
  • Google Authentication: Secure login with Google OAuth via Firebase
  • Mark as Eaten: Track the foods you've tried and watch your food collection grow - unlocking a satisfying sense of progress and discovering your evolving tastes along the way.
  • Favorites: Save your favorite dishes for quick access
  • Food Details: Detailed pages for each food with comprehensive nutrition information, nutrition facts, plus videos and tasty recommendations from food bloggers.
  • Progress Tracking: Track your collection progress with stats and completion percentages. Hit milestones by collecting dishes across rarity levels to receive celebratory moments and optional shareable progress posters for social media.
  • Mobile-Friendly: Fully responsive design that works on all devices
