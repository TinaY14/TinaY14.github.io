üî¨ Fermentation Process

Steps:

  1. Rice Preparation: Rinse glutinous rice until water runs clear, soak for 4-6 hours
  2. Steaming: Steam rice for 30-40 minutes until translucent and tender
  3. Cooling: Cool steamed rice to room temperature (around 25-30¬∞C)
  4. Yeast Addition: Crush wine yeast balls and mix thoroughly with cooled rice
  5. Fermentation: Pack mixture in sterilized jar, create a well in center
  6. Aging: Cover and ferment at room temperature for 3-7 days
  7. Straining: Filter through cheesecloth to separate liquid wine

Results & Insights: Successfully produced clear, aromatic rice wine with a pleasant sweet-tart flavor. The project deepened my appreciation for traditional fermentation techniques and their scientific principles. The process taught me about sterile technique, temperature control, and the importance of timing in fermentation science.

Cultural Connection: Rice wine has been produced in China for over 4,000 years and plays an important role in Chinese cuisine and culture, often used in cooking and traditional celebrations.
