About Me.

My name is Xitian (Tina) Yu, and I'm an Engineering Chemistry student at Stony Brook University with an expected graduation in 2029. I have a deep passion for nutrition and its impact on health and wellness, which has led me to actively engage with organizations like the American Society of Nutrition and Nutrition Facts Organization.

What excites me most about nutrition is that it combines science with practical everyday choices. I enjoy learning how biology and physiology come together to explain how nutrients are absorbed and used in the body. At the same time, I find it inspiring to see how this knowledge can be applied in real life—whether through meal planning, public health education, or supporting individuals in reaching their health goals.
